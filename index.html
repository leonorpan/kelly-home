Help Kelly find a forever home!

Hello kind people, my name is Kelly!

I am a girl approximately 8 months old, born as a stray kitten in a neighboorhood of Thessaloniki, in north Greece.

One day when I was still very small, around 3 months old, I became very sick, and had a very high fever and I would have died alone.

But a kind lady took me in her hands and gave me medication and saved me from certain death.

In my country, Greece it was too hard to find a home, because there are kittens like me everywhere, so this lady took me to Germany, and I believe here in Berlin I have better changes of finding a home.

Now I am staying with a girl who fosters me but has two other cats, so I can't stay here forever.

I get along very well with all cats, in Greece I was living with 3 others cats in a small apartment and now in Berlin I stay with 2 other cats, never causing a problem and I make friends fast.

I like to meow and be chatty and have conversations with everyone, especially humans.

Sometimes I am a little bit jealous and I want to be petted a lot.

Although I grew up outside in the streets, I don't really like to go out of the apartment, but I love to stay indoors.

I believe I can be a good match if you already have another cat, as I love having company and play with other cats!
